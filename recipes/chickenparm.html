Viking Chicken Parmesan

This tasty looking dish can only be consumed by only the most worthy and respectable vikings. Most believe that Chicken Parmesan was originally first made by the humble Italians, but this is wrong. So if you have just 44 minutues and the desire tribute to Odin and Thor keep reading.

Things Needed

  • 2 Large eggs
  • Minced Galric
  • 3 Large Chicken breasts
  • Sauce (Blood of thy enemies)
  • Bread crumbs
  • Frying Pan and Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions

  1. Beast chicken breasts with Thors' Hammer
  2. Beat two large eggs with Odin's whisk, then dredge beaten chicken breasts
  3. Coat chicken with bread crumbs
  4. Sear breasts in frying pan with generous amounts of oil
  5. Place seared breasts in oven pre-heated to 400 degrees for 15 minutes
  6. remove from oven and coat with Sauce and cover with Parmesan Cheese
  7. Bake for another 5 minutes
